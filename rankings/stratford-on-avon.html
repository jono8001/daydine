The score, briefly.

Trust & Compliance · 40%
FSA/FHRS hygiene rating, structural compliance, confidence in management, inspection recency. Public-register data; older inspections decay over time.
Customer Validation · 45%
Bayesian-shrunk ratings from Google, TripAdvisor and OpenTable. The "Sources" column indicates whether independent platforms agree on the venue.
Commercial Readiness · 15%
Is the venue set up to trade? Online presence, hours completeness, reservation and delivery paths, photo coverage, Companies House signals.
Confidence gating
