How This Score Works

The DayDine Restaurant Confidence Score (RCS) is a composite 0–10 metric derived from 40 signals across six weighted tiers: FSA compliance, Google presence, TripAdvisor, operational capability, menu & offering, and editorial reputation. Scores are computed monthly from publicly observable data — no internal systems required.

Rating Bands

  • Excellent — 8.0 to 10.0
  • Good — 6.5 to 7.9
  • Generally Satisfactory — 5.0 to 6.4
  • Improvement Necessary — 3.5 to 4.9
  • Major Improvement — 2.0 to 3.4
  • Urgent Improvement — below 2.0
