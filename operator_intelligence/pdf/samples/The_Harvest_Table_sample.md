## Appendix A — Methodology Note

This report uses the DayDine Restaurant Confidence Score (RCS) v3.4. The headline 0–10 score is a weighted composite of 40 signals across six active tiers (Food Safety, Google, Online Presence, Operational, Menu & Offering, Reputation & Awards) plus penalty rules drawn from Companies House status. Temporal decay is applied to FSA inspection age and review recency. Cross-source convergence is checked pairwise across Google, Tripadvisor and FSA — converged ratings receive a small bonus, divergent ratings a small penalty.

For the full methodology see daydine.com/methodology.

## Appendix B — Glossary

- **RCS** — Restaurant Confidence Score, the headline 0–10 metric.
- **GBP** — Google Business Profile.
- **FSA** — Food Standards Agency hygiene rating.
- **P-rank** — percentile rank within the relevant peer set.
- **Convergence** — agreement between independent rating sources (Google, Tripadvisor, FSA). Converged sources reinforce confidence; divergent sources flag investigation.
