How DayDine keeps it simple.

You should not need to understand a scoring model to choose dinner. DayDine reduces the data into three questions: is the venue trusted, is it well validated by customers, and is it easy to use in the real world?

1 · Trusted? Hygiene and compliance evidence. Public FSA/FHRS data helps separate food-safety confidence from ordinary review hype.
2 · Well validated? Customer rating and review-count metadata. A small number of glowing reviews is treated cautiously; stronger samples carry more confidence.
3 · Easy to choose? Opening, menu and contact visibility. We reward venues that are easy to check, contact and book from public information.
