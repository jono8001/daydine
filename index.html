How It Works

From public signals to market position

01

Collect

We monitor public data continuously — food safety inspections, review platforms, business filings, operational signals — and normalise it into comparable metrics.

02

Score

The Restaurant Confidence Score synthesises 40 signals across 7 independent tiers into a single 0–10 metric. Cross-source convergence is measured. Thin data is flagged, not hidden.

03

Rank

Every venue in a covered market gets a unique position relative to its local peers. Scores update weekly. The methodology is published. The formula is not.
