Inside the report · Six panels

What a Position & Competitor report contains.

No critic notes. No opinions. The RCS V4 data that drives your public rank, decomposed for your team.
01 · Position

Your rank inside your local authority, with a monthly RCS trend line.

Where you sit today, where you sat, and how far you've moved month-over-month. With band transitions (Excellent → Good, or the reverse) flagged.

02 · Components

The three weighted inputs that make up your RCS V4 score.

Trust & Compliance (40%), Customer Validation (45%), Commercial Readiness (15%) — each decomposed into its constituent signals, each contribution quantified.

03 · Peers

The five closest venues by RCS in your category, in your market.

Pub, Cafe, Restaurant or Hotel peer-set as it actually appears in the data. Who moved past you, who you moved past, and on which component it happened.

04 · Local landscape

The full local-market ranking, not just your slice.

New entrants, closures (FSA deletions), and Companies House ownership changes detected in your local authority this reporting period.

05 · Convergence

Which public sources agree on you — and which one is the outlier.

When FSA, Google and TripAdvisor point in different directions, the report names the outlier and quantifies the split. Convergence is a score in its own right.

06 · Outlook

Non-prescriptive. What would need to move for you to move.

We do not tell you what to cook, how to price, or how to staff. We tell you which component is currently capping your RCS and by how many points a one-band gain would require.

The questions operators actually ask.

Does buying a report move my ranking?

No. Subscribers and non-subscribers are processed by the same scoring pipeline, with the same public inputs, on the same schedule. The reports team reads rankings after they are locked.

Can I see my report before the public ranking updates?

No. Operator reports ship within 48 hours of the public ranking refreshing, never before. We do not share draft positions with anyone, ever.

What if I disagree with my placement?

Subscribers get a priority correction review. If a datum is wrong — a wrongly-attributed FSA record, a merged Google listing, a stale postcode — we correct it across the whole ranking, not just your report.

Can I buy a report for a competitor?

No. Reports are sold only to the operator of the venue in question, verified via Companies House. Competitive context appears inside your own report; you can't commission one for someone else.

Is the data the same as what the public ranking uses?

Yes. The full RCS V4 pipeline — FSA/FHRS, customer-platform ratings, Companies House, Google Business signals — is the same dataset, presented operator-side with sub-signal decomposition.

Do you sell window badges or awards?

No. Ranked venues get a window sticker, free of charge. You cannot buy a badge. You cannot buy a ranking. You can buy an explanation.

My venue is in a market that isn't live yet — can I still get a report?

Partially. We can generate an FSA-only Pre-Launch Report for any UK local authority using our 264,791-establishment dataset. Full RCS V4 reports require the market to be on the live pipeline. Ask us and we'll prioritise your area.
